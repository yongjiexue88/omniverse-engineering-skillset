# Omniverse Ideate

Use this skill when creativity needs engineering constraints.

## Workflow

1. Name the audience, job-to-be-done, and constraints.
2. Generate distinct concepts rather than minor variations.
3. Score concepts against usefulness, feasibility, clarity, and risk.
4. Pick the strongest direction and explain why.
5. Convert it into a testable next step.

## Output Shape

Use a compact table:

| Concept | What it is | Why it could work | Cost | Risk |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Then include:

- `Recommendation`
- `Prototype path`
- `What to validate first`

## Rules

- Avoid ideas that only differ in surface wording.
- Favor concepts that can be validated with a small artifact.
- Keep implementation feasibility visible.
